Technology

Built on Cutting-Edge AI

Every feature designed for transparency, accuracy and fairness

🤖 AI Credit Scoring
XGBoost model trained on 50+ features delivers accurate credit risk scores with 94.2% accuracy.
🔍 Explainable AI (XAI)
SHAP-based explanations show exactly which factors drove the decision — full transparency guaranteed.
🚨 Anomaly Detection
Isolation Forest algorithm flags suspicious patterns, income mismatches and potential fraud in real time.
👤 Human-in-the-Loop
Borderline cases (score 580–699) are escalated to a human credit officer ensuring no unfair automation.
✅
Rule-Based Validation
8+ RBI and SBI compliance rules auto-validate PAN, Aadhaar, income ratios and loan eligibility.
📧
Gmail Notifications
Real-time email alerts for application submission, AI decision, document verification and EMI reminders.
🧮
EMI Calculator
Interactive planner with full amortization schedule, interest breakup and visual principal vs interest split.
💬
AI Chatbot
24×7 assistant answers queries on eligibility, documents, interest rates, loan process and more.
